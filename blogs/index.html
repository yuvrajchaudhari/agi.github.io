​Taking A Leap From Digitalization to Digital Automation

March 17, 2021 by Red Bixbite Solutions

Businesses going online can be referred to as going digital, Transforming the cultural Business practices and adopting the latest computing versions can be considered as going digital. Paperless work practices and cloud storage are called to be going digital. But just simple optimization of digital platforms is no longer cognitive enough and comprehensive, End-to-end Automation offers actual growth across the entire value chain system.

Whether by need or demand, digital Automation took center stage for several businesses in 2020. The pandemic and succeeding lockdowns, restrictions, and therefore have to be compelled to still serve customers forced business house owners and decision-makers to rethink however they act with customers, a way to incorporate contactless models into their workflows and the way to stay customers and workers connected and engaged with one another. The immediate response for many businesses was to hunt out prebuilt collaboration tools or services to support the move to virtual interactions and still deliver services to customers. Before the pandemic, the foremost common form of interaction between most businesses and their customers was direct, face to face, with solely a couple of businesses partaking with customers just about via video. For these scattered virtual interactions and tiny internal conferences, the meeting expertise delivered by a canned application was usually sufficient. The pandemic flipped the script: in several cases, business interactions were emotional nearly utterly to virtual channels, generally with very little warning. This move to all-virtual interactions highlighted the constraints of meeting-centric tools. As workflows and business models become even slightly more complicated or nuanced, the willned application expertise can become a burden instead of an associate degree enabler.

Digital Automation could be a journey that organizations should undertake to confirm that they still keep relevant. Organizations should amend however they manage their individuals, things, processes and knowledge to create the choices which will contour their operations, scale back prices and adopt new business models to support client growth. Ancient firms that don't initiate efforts to remodel their organizations and use digital business models risk devolution and failure to keep up incumbency in their business. As their customers evolve, organizations should evolve with them to still meet their desires.

Adopting and adapting to digital has to be an anticipatory thought in business today. The business demands to adapt as we can't run away from the fact that changes are happening around anyway. Many businesses are digitally deprived as they couldn't compete with the updated market, they have already fallen behind, the pace is crucial to becoming a leader.

So, let's not make a mistake comparing Digital transformation with updating technology. Digital transformation ain't only updating with new technology but also reaching out to the customer's expectations and giving them a sense of satisfaction, agile, becoming more customer-focused to survive in the competitive market.
